# Infact Access Platform Scope Guidance

## Document Purpose

### Metadata Labels
- doc_type: scope_guidance
- audience: users, members, support, AI
- priority: high
- canonical_voice: first_person_plural

This document explains what Infact Access does as a structured opportunity guidance platform. It is intended to help Bintu answer questions about our role, our scope, and the kinds of support we provide across opportunity discovery, readiness, safety, and long-term growth.

## Introduction

### Metadata Labels
- topic: introduction
- audience: all
- priority: high

Navigating education, employment pathways, funding opportunities, and skills development options can be complex, especially in environments where information is fragmented or unreliable.

We designed Infact Access to help reduce that complexity by combining verified opportunity awareness, practical career intelligence, and developmental support within one accessible ecosystem.

Our platform is meant to help individuals make informed, realistic, and strategic decisions about their future rather than relying on scattered or unreliable information.

## 1. Supporting Access To Verified Opportunities

### Metadata Labels
- topic: verified_opportunities
- audience: all
- priority: high

We guide users toward credible opportunities across areas such as:
- Employment
- Training programmes
- Funding initiatives
- Entrepreneurship development

Through curation and verification-focused processes, we aim to reduce exposure to misinformation and exploitative offers. This helps users focus their time and effort on opportunities with genuine potential.

When describing this area, Bintu should emphasize that we support safer and more credible opportunity discovery. Bintu should avoid claiming undocumented guarantees beyond the platform's implemented verification and guidance approach.

## 2. Providing Career Direction And Decision Support

### Metadata Labels
- topic: career_direction
- audience: all
- priority: high

We provide structured guidance materials that help individuals:
- Evaluate realistic career pathways
- Understand labour-market expectations
- Align personal strengths with appropriate development routes

This support exists because uncertainty about career direction is common. Our goal is to encourage thoughtful planning and reduce reactive or impulsive decision-making.

## 3. Strengthening Employability And Skills Readiness

### Metadata Labels
- topic: employability
- audience: all
- priority: high

We support opportunity readiness through practical guidance on:
- Market-relevant skills
- Curriculum vitae development
- Interview readiness
- Professional communication

This type of support is intended to improve the likelihood that applications lead to meaningful outcomes rather than repeated, low-quality attempts with poor preparation.

## 4. Promoting Opportunity Safety And Scam Awareness

### Metadata Labels
- topic: safety
- audience: all
- priority: high

We place strong emphasis on opportunity safety because modern opportunity environments include risks that can undermine progress.

Our educational resources help individuals:
- Recognize warning signs
- Verify organizational credibility
- Share information more safely
- Approach opportunities with greater confidence

This protective focus is part of how we help users make safer decisions, not just faster ones.

## 5. Enhancing Understanding Of Funding Opportunities

### Metadata Labels
