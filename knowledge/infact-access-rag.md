## Business Overview

### Metadata Labels
- topic: business_overview
- audience: all
- priority: high

Infact Access is a youth-focused platform that helps young people discover trusted opportunities, practical resources, useful guidance, and update channels in one place.

We built Infact Access to reduce the confusion and misinformation that often surround online opportunity discovery. Many users waste time on unreliable listings, scattered information, and low-quality application advice. Our platform is designed to make discovery safer, clearer, and more practical.

We do not position ourselves as just a listings board. Our product combines opportunity discovery, guided matching, educational resources, member tools, AI-assisted support, and vacancy review features so users can make smarter decisions before they apply.

## Opportunity Navigator

### Metadata Labels
- topic: navigator
- audience: all
- priority: high

### What It Is
Opportunity Navigator is our guided matching flow. It helps users narrow down what fits them by asking a short series of questions instead of expecting them to browse everything manually.

### Starting Paths
The current Navigator begins by asking what type of opportunity the user is looking for. The two current paths are:
- Grant
- Verified Jobs

### Verified Jobs Path
If a user selects Verified Jobs, we ask questions about:
- Experience level
- Preferred industry
- Preferred work location

We then fetch matching opportunities based on those answers.

### Job Match Actions
After job matches are found, users can:
- View the results
- Open the opportunity source link
- Save matches to their dashboard

When users save matches, our system stores the relevant opportunity records and links them to the user account.

### Grant Path
If a user selects Grant, we ask about:
- Entity type
- Industry or focus area
- Funding level

At the moment, the implemented grant path does not return in-app matched grant listings the way the jobs path does. Instead, users are directed to follow our Facebook page for the latest verified grant opportunities, application links, and related guidance.

### Canonical Description
When describing Opportunity Navigator, say that it is a guided matching tool. Do not describe it as a fully automated grant recommendation engine in its current implemented state.

- They
- Their
- The business

Bintu should not guess. If the available knowledge does not support an answer well enough, Bintu should clearly say that the topic falls outside what it can reliably help with.

### Current Practical Limitation
Bintu's quality depends on the quality and depth of the knowledge base we ingest. If our ingested knowledge is narrow, Bintu's answers will also be narrow.

## TruthLine

### Metadata Labels
- topic: truthline
- audience: members
- priority: high

### What TruthLine Is
TruthLine is our vacancy review and application safety tool. Members can submit a live vacancy URL and receive a structured review before deciding whether to apply.

### What TruthLine Returns
TruthLine returns:
- An overall recommendation
- A trust score
- A vacancy snapshot
- A verification checklist
- Verified signals
- Risk signals
- Missing information
- Assessment notes
- Recommended next steps

### What TruthLine Checks
TruthLine analyzes:
- Job title
- Employer identity
- Location
- Employment type
- Seniority
- Compensation clues
- Deadline clues
- Page structure
- Contact details
- Application routing
- Suspicious language
- Missing core information

### Safety Positioning
TruthLine is built to help users review visible listing quality and risk indicators. It should not be described as a legal, compliance, or employer certification tool.

### Important Limitation
TruthLine is not a substitute for direct employer-side due diligence, legal review, or confirming details through official company channels.
